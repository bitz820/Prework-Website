About Me

WHO ARE YOU?

I am a budding full-stack Software Engineer with a background in Musical Theatre. I am adventurous and love to take on things I have never done before. I live by the motto that every day is a chance to learn something I didn't know the day before. This has manifested itself in many ways!


  • A successful NYC Musical Theatre career

  • Working as the only American aboard a German Cruise Line

  • Buying, remodeling/rennovating, traveling in, and then flipping Motorhomes

I am always on the lookout for something to push me out of my comfort zone!

WHY THE SWITCH TO SOFTWARE ENGINEERING?

I have always loved puzzles! I am looking to expand my creative endeavors and solve complex problems by reading, writing, debugging, and rendering code for practical application.

After the pandemic forced a halt of gathering in large groups, I was interested in diversifying my opportunities. I love to take a challenge and put my own spin on it. I am a creative individual with a problem-solving mindset so this seemed like a natural next step for me.

WHERE DO YOU TRAIN? WHAT DO YOU KNOW?

I received my Bachelor of Fine Arts from THE Florida State University (GO NOLES!). As far as programming is concerned, I began as a self-taught programmer going through both the Odin Project as well as Free Code Camp before settling on a BootCamp to really test my stamina and take me to the next level. I am currently enrolled in the Software Engineering Program at Flatiron School in New York City.


I am familiar with HTML, CSS, Javascript, and Ruby as well as the React and Rails Frameworks. I am always looking to learn more; after all, it's not the language you know, rather the understanding of how to solve the problem in any language.

Enough about me... Let's hop to it!
